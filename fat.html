Calculation Method

This calculator uses the U.S. Navy Body Fat Formula, which is based on circumference measurements.

For Men:

%Fat = 495 / (1.0324 - 0.19077 * log10(waist - neck) + 0.15456 * log10(height)) - 450

For Women:

%Fat = 495 / (1.29579 - 0.35004 * log10(waist + hip - neck) + 0.221 * log10(height)) - 450

Health & Fitness Tips

  • Aim for gradual fat loss (0.5-1kg per week)
  • Combine cardio and strength training
  • Focus on whole, unprocessed foods
  • Stay hydrated (3-4L water daily)
  • Get 7-9 hours of sleep nightly
  • Manage stress through meditation or yoga
